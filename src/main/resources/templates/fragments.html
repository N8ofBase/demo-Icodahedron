Welcome to the Icosahedron

It is an impressive, gigantic, polyhedron with 20 sides of equilateral 60 degree triangles. This one contains it's own universe where the actions of the beings inhabiting it are determined by the roll of their own personal icosahedron. It is not an easy life, relying on the luck of a twenty sided die, but it is the only life they know. Currently the Icosahedron is decaying as a result of the Crit Castle falling under corrupt rule causing a plague of "1's" to affect all the creatures, produce, and resources. The land will soon perish if balance is not restored. Your icosahedron seems to be resistant to the corrupt energy of the Castle. Not only that, but it has granted you special abilities. It seems you are the Hero the Folks have needed to embark on a perilous and unlucky journey to erase the corruption and restore prosperity to all beings in The Icosahedron.
